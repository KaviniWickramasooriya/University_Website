Horizon Campus is a leading private university in Sri Lanka geared to provide an inspiring learning experience to students within a holistic university atmosphere. The Campus opened its doors marking a cornerstone in the national educational sector of the country, with the aim of delivering a fully-fledged generation to the world of tomorrow. Horizon Campus offers UGC approved 15 accredited degree courses under the five faculties, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Information Technology, Faculty of International Programs, Faculty of Management and Faculty of Science. Every year, more than 1200 students get enrolled in the academic programmes for Sri Lankan degrees as well as the degrees offered by top universities in UK, USA, and Asia.

We are proud to be approved by the Ministry of Higher Education, University Grants Commission, Association of CommonWealth, Asia Pacific Quality Network and University of London. The TRIPLE ACCREDITED status of Horizon Campus and other recognition, guarantees you of a nationally and internationally recognized qualification.

...Life at Horizon...

“ Horizon Campus offers world-class education to students, with the launch of a next-generation multi-story, multi-space university facility at the “Knowledge City Malabe’ (KCM) themed as “a new city, a new life. ”



LIBRARY

Horizon Campus library houses updated collections of books, editorials, and journals in the fields of Law, Science, Management, IT, Education, Engineering and many more. All Horizon Campus students are granted membership at the time of registration with the campus and can access a large variety of literature for education and relaxation free of charge. With the use of modern technology these are available for students via the e – library facility that can be accessed from anywhere across the world.

TRANSPORT

While the campus itself is located in a public and private transport accessible area, a shuttle service is arranged to and from Malabe, Pannipitiya, Maharagama, Nugegoda, Dehiwala, Homagama, and Godagama. Students can use these services free of charge..

ACCOMODATION

• Fully furnished rooms with bed, table, chairs, wardrobe and towel rack with attached bathroom on individual or sharing basis • Private and common study areas • Surrounded by calm and natural environment conducive for studying and relaxing • Laundry facilities within the premises • Hostel warden within the premises • CCTV monitoring systems and 24x7 security

COMPUTER LABS

The computer labs are equipped with high tech equipment available for the use of students and connected to high speed internet to support academic work. These can accommodate up to 100 students at a time.

MODERN CLASS ROOMS

Classrooms and lecture halls are designed to facilitate all academic needs of students and are on par with the international university standards.

CAFETERIA

The on campus cafeteria is managed by Perera and Sons and offers local and international cuisine at affordable rates.

SCIENCE LABS

Horizon Campus Science Labs; Chemistry Lab, Physics Lab, Microbiology Lab, Molecular Biology Lab, Plant Tissue Culture Lab are well equipped with up-to-date and comprehensive gear for experimenting, research and development. All labs are maintained by Lab technicians and students are expected to use them according to assigned schedules.

AUDITORIUM

The state – of – the – art auditorium is fully furnished with modern audio visual equipment and can house 400 at a time. This is available for all academic and non academic purposes upon prior approval.

BANKING SERVICE

With convenience in mind for the students, Horizon Campus has facilitated ATM machines from different banks within its premises.

POOL AND GROUND

GYM

Horizon Campus gym is available for both students and staff upon obtaining a membership card at an affordable cost. It is open from 4.45PM to 6.30 PM during weekdays and is facilitated with personal trainers.

RECREATIONAL

Student recreational areas comprise of a swimming pool, cricket grounds with pavilion, lounge, and a pond accessible to nature with an arena, volleyball court and basketball courts.
